“Study Chakra” has been established by some highly motivated, qualified, passionate and experienced teachers with a clear vision of revolutionizing the field of education by striving hard to carve out best even from a lay student.

Conducive teaching methodology and proper planning have always proved to be cornerstone for excelling in the field of studies and we have focused on the same.

Our teaching experiences, well designed teaching pattern and best prepared study material made after a long and meticulous study and analysis of the respective subjects and IIT-JEE Main/Advanced & NEET/AIIMS exam patterns pave the way for the students to turn their grand dreams into reality of getting into prestigious engineering and medical institutes such as IITs, NITs, AIIMS etc.

The institute has been built with the aim of incorporating newest ideas and features in the teaching methodology to produce best results and not merely sticking to conventional teaching techniques.

We are well equipped with qualified and experienced teachers. Apart from highly planned study material we have provided library facilities with collection of various standard reference books for the respective subjects to stimulate the aspirants to strive only for the best.

We have also focused on the enhancement of learning through technology as per the requirement. There will be earnest efforts from all the teachers with complete dedication to make sure the aspirants successfully achieve their goals.

Key Features

Our team toiled hard to come up with the best and comprehensive study material with well researched and fully analyzed content keeping in mind the pattern of IIT-JEE Main/Advanced & NEET/AIIMS entrance exams. Study material has been designed in such a way that not only in entrance exams but also students can excel in board exams.

Library facility well equipped with standard reference books are provided to the students. Students can issue the books and study on a regular basis.

There will be tests on each topic on the regular basis to keep a track of performances by students. The tests are of various types, formats and difficulty levels to have a complete analysis for the improvement of student’s performance. From now onwards, JEE Main exams will be computer based called Computer Based Test (CBT) only. We have computer based online tests those will be conducted on a regular basis to have a good practice of CBT and finally there will be full length tests at the end of the course completely based on the actual pattern of IIT-JEE Main/Advanced & NEET/AIIMS exams.

In order to maximize the efficiency of lectures, student to teacher ratio plays a very important role. There will be one teacher per 25 students in a class, that is, the capacity of one batch will be 25 students. This ratio makes very convenient environment for both students and teachers in the class.

Students will be counselled on a regular basis to listen to their problems they are facing regarding their preparations and to find the solutions for these problems. They will be trained to focus on required areas of respective subjects. There will be regular motivational counselling to keep them motivated throughout the duration of the course.

Doubt sessions are arranged at the end of each topic in which students can ask their doubts regarding the topics completed in the class. After having solved all the doubts of the students of the topic, next topic will be started.

There is facility of CCTV classroom surveillance. We have CCTV placed in each classroom, corridor, office as well as library. Students and parents are sent SMS alerts regarding tests, lectures and other sessions. Notifications will also be sent through e-mails. There will be biometric attendance of students.

Based on tests and classroom response student’s performances are analyzed on a regular basis. Information regarding performances will be conveyed to parents through e-mails and SMS. Parents meeting will be conducted on a regular basis to inform them regarding the growth in performance of the students.

We have used technology in order to obtain better output and increase the efficiency of learning. Classrooms are provided with projectors and CPUs. Ceilings are fitted with speakers. Students will be taught through videos, powerpoint presentations, pictures and animations wherever required so that they have clear visualization of concepts taught. Online portal will be provided where students can ask their doubts.
